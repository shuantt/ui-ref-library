Why E-Ink Design Matters

In a world of saturated colors and overwhelming visual noise, there's profound beauty in simplicity. E-ink design strips away everything unnecessary, leaving only the essential: black ink on white paper.

"The absence of color is not a limitation—it's a liberation."

This aesthetic draws from centuries of print design wisdom. The Kindle, Remarkable tablet, and newspaper layouts all embrace these principles: generous whitespace, readable typography, and restrained visual hierarchy.

CORE PRINCIPLES

  1. 01. Black and white only. Gray sparingly.
  2. 02. Typography carries the design.
  3. 03. Whitespace is not empty space.
  4. 04. Every element must earn its place.
